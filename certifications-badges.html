Skillshare · Growth & Retention · 2024

Certifications & Badges

I drive measurable business impact.

The Business Problem

Students weren't finishing courses. Project submission rate the behavior most tied to retention was low. There was no meaningful reward for completing the work, so most learners stopped before the finish line.

What We Built

A full certification journey discovery → progress tracking → completion → celebration → share.

The certificate itself was a shareable artifact earned by completing a course and submitting a teacher-assigned project. Not just a badge proof of real work done.

The Unexpected Move

Sharing was originally out of scope. I pushed for it as a fast follow because sharing behavior is directly tied to referral growth.

More importantly, I designed the certificate page itself as a referral entry point. When a student shares their certificate, the page a new visitor lands on is their introduction to Skillshare. The certificate page does double duty: recognition for the earner, acquisition for the platform.

Process

Four rounds of iteration including Figma prototype user testing before launch. Testing early revealed where the emotional arc broke down moments that felt bureaucratic instead of celebratory and allowed us to fix them before shipping. Measured via A/B test post-launch.

Outcome

+89%

Increase in project submission rate the key behavior tied to long-term retention. Measured via A/B test.
